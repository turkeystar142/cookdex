## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 2 cups flour
- 1 3/4 cups milk
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. **Mix all ingredients** - In a large bowl, whisk together all ingredients until smooth.

2. **Cook on waffle iron** - Pour batter into a preheated waffle iron and cook according to manufacturer's instructions until golden and crispy.

## Tips

- Don't overmix the batter - a few lumps are okay.
- Let the waffle iron fully preheat for crispier waffles.
- Serve with butter, syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.
