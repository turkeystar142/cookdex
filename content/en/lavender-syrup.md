## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups water
- 1 cup sugar (ratio, boil to simple syrup)
- 2 tablespoons lavender (dried flowers)
- 1/2 lime juice, after straining and reheating

## Instructions

1. **Make simple syrup** - Combine water and sugar in a saucepan. Bring to a boil until sugar dissolves.

2. **Add lavender** - Add lavender flowers and let steep for 5-10 minutes.

3. **Strain** - Strain out the lavender flowers.

4. **Add lime juice** - Reheat the syrup and add lime juice. Stir well.

## Tips

- Use culinary lavender, not decorative lavender.
- Don't steep too long or hot, it will become bitter.
- Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
- Great for cocktails, lemonade, or in royal icing.
