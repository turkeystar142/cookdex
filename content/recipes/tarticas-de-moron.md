Traditional Cuban shortbread cookies with lime zest.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cups shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons lime zest

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** - Preheat oven to 350°F.

2. **Cream sugar and shortening** - Mix sugar and shortening until smooth.

3. **Add flour and zest** - When smooth, add flour and lime zest. Mix until well combined.

4. **Shape and bake** - Shape into cookies and place on a greased pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes.

5. **Cool** - Let cool completely until solidified.

6. **Finish** - Sprinkle sugar on top.

## Tips

- These cookies have a delicate, crumbly texture.
- Don't overbake - they should be lightly golden.
- Store in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
