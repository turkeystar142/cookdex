## Ingredients

- 100 g chocolate
- 50 g butter
- 2 eggs
- 20 g sugar
- 30 g flour

## Instructions

1. **Melt chocolate and butter** - Melt chocolate and butter together and mix until smooth.

2. **Beat eggs with sugar** - Separately, beat eggs with sugar until combined.

3. **Combine** - Add chocolate mix to egg mix and stir well.

4. **Add flour** - Add flour to the chocolate-egg mix and fold until just combined.

5. **Bake** - Pour into a greased pan or muffin tin and bake for 10 minutes at 180°C (~355°F).

6. **Serve** - Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto a plate. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top.

## Tips

- Don't overbake - the center should be molten.
- Serve immediately for the best lava effect.
- Great with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
