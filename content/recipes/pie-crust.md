Flaky and buttery pie crust that's perfect for any pie.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 cup flour
- 1/4 tablespoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 lb butter (cold, cubed)
- 3 1/2 tablespoons ice water

## Instructions

1. **Pulse dry ingredients and butter** - In a food processor, pulse the flour, sugar, salt, and cold cubed butter until pea-sized pieces form.

2. **Add water** - Slowly add ice water and pulse until the dough comes together.

3. **Form and roll** - Form into a ball and roll out on a floured surface.

## Tips

- Keep the butter cold for the flakiest crust.
- Don't overwork the dough.
- Chill the dough for 30 minutes before rolling if time permits.
