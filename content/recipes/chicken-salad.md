Creamy chicken salad with celery, green onion, and Dijon mustard.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup/can cooked chicken shreds
- 1/4 cup mayo
- 1/2 rib celery
- 1 green onion
- 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon dill
- 1/2 cup green apple, cubed (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Mix everything together** - Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix until well combined.

2. **Serve** - Serve alone or use for sandwiches.

## Tips

- Add the green apple for a sweet and crunchy contrast.
- Great for sandwiches, wraps, or on crackers.
- Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
