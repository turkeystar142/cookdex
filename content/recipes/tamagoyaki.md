Japanese rolled omelette with a sweet and savory flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon mirin
- 2 pinches pepper
- 3 eggs
- Oil (in pan)

## Instructions

1. **Mix ingredients** - Mix all ingredients together. Do not overmix, no bubbles.

2. **Heat pan** - Heat a square pan and add oil.

3. **Pour first layer** - Pour a thin layer of the egg mixture into the hot oiled square pan.

4. **Add toppings** - Add toppings if applicable.

5. **Roll** - Roll the first layer tightly, then push to one side. Add more egg mixture and roll again.

6. **Repeat** - Continue until you've used all the egg mixture or reached desired size.

## Tips

- Use a rectangular tamagoyaki pan for the traditional shape.
- Optional toppings include green onions, nori, or cheese.
- Serve sliced with rice and other Japanese dishes.
